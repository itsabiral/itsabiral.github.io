title: office havoc, a corporate life simulator game
tldr: a 2d side scoller made for ludum dare game jam using unity, c#, photoshop in 48 hrs
date: 2020-10-03
---
this game is about the monotonous daily routine of a worker working in a software company where he is assigned with some task given by his austere boss. you’ll be the worker and do the tasks assigned by the boss and complete it. if you couldn’t complete the tasks then you’ll be fired so you are “Stuck in a loop” of tasks given by the boss. <br>

drink coffee, boost your energy, complete the given tasks and get paid!! <br>

**ludum dare 47 - Oct. 3, 2020**<br>
**tools used: unity, c#, photoshop for art**<br>
**team: me & prerak**
