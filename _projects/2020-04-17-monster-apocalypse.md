monster apocalypse is a top down shooter game made for ludum dare game jam<br>

in this game you play as a monkey to kill monsters who are trying to capture the forest. your goal is to save your friends from those monsters. you can throw bananas from the awesome banana gun (lol) to kill the enemies.<br>

**ludum dare 46 - April 17, 2020**<br>
**tools used: unity, c#, photoshop for art**
